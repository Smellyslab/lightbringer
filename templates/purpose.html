Why Do The Lightbringers Exist?


The Lightbringers exist because we belive that knowledge should not be limited by governmental and societal beliefs and norms.
We belive that people should be able to know anything they want no matter its legality or societal status.
Shed light upon the unknown.
Be a Lightbringer.


Who and what are the Lightbringers?
The Lightbringers are a Luciferian anti-society group based around the belief of freedom of all knowledge and the belief in the destruction of society and corrupt governments.
